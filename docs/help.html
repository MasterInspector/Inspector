Instructions

_________________________________________

Loading Images

● Drag and drop one or multiple images into Image Group 1 or Image Group 2.

● You can load one or both groups.

● Switch between groups to compare images hitting "Tab" key .

● You can reload any set of images in any Image Group at any time, just drag the images.

Navigating Images

● Use the ◄ and ► buttons or Arrow Left ⬅️ / Arrow Right ➡️ keys.

Switching Groups

● Press "Tab" to toggle between Group 1 and Group 2.

● You can select them with mouse click too.

● The active Group is highlighted.

Zoom & Pan

● Use your mouse scroll wheel  over the image to zoom in and out.

● Drag the image with any mouse button to pan around.

● All images will keep the same zoom and position for easy comparison.

● Zoom Factor: Use mouse wheal  over the slider to set the level of Zoom Factor.

● Hit "R" to reset zoom and position.

Moving the Control Panel

● Click and drag the control panel to reposition it anywhere on screen.

● Press "H" to toggle Hide / Unhide the control panel.
